Why I deleted Tiktok and why you should too

Have you noticed lately that you cannot concentrate for extended periods of time as you once could in the past? Do you lack the motivation to do anything, even the things you once enjoyed? If your answer is yes, do not worry, for you are not alone. Many individuals are suffering from a lack of concentration and motivation in today's world. In today's blog, I will provide you with an explanation for this phenomenon.

In recent years, social media platforms have become the go-to source of entertainment for many people around the world. One such platform that has exploded in popularity in recent times is TikTok. With its short, snappy videos and endless scroll, it's easy to lose hours of your day scrolling through content.

As someone who enjoyed using TikTok, I was no exception to this trend. However, over time, I began to feel that my time spent on the app was becoming increasingly unproductive. I was consuming so much content, yet I wasn't learning anything new or expanding my knowledge.

That's when I made the decision to delete the app and start reading instead. Reading has always been a passion of mine, but like many others, I had started to neglect it in favor of more immediate forms of entertainment. But now that I have picked up the habit again, I can confidently say that it has changed my life in several ways.

Firstly, reading has expanded my knowledge and broadened my horizons. When I read a book, I'm transported to a new world, introduced to new ideas, and challenged to think in new ways. It has given me a fresh perspective on life and the world around me.

In contrast, TikTok, with its 15-second videos, only gave me a shallow glimpse into the lives of others. I was only seeing snippets of their lives and experiences, and never getting the full picture. In contrast, reading has allowed me to dive deep into the stories and experiences of others, giving me a fuller understanding of the world around me.

Secondly, reading has helped me reduce stress and improve my mental health. Studies have shown that reading can reduce stress levels and help with anxiety and depression. When I'm reading a book, I'm not thinking about the stresses of everyday life. Instead, I'm focused on the story, allowing my mind to relax and unwind.

On the other hand, TikTok can be overwhelming, with its constant stream of information and entertainment. It can be difficult to switch off and take a break when you're always being bombarded with new content.

Lastly, reading has helped me improve my concentration and focus. With the constant stream of notifications and distractions on social media, it can be difficult to focus on anything for an extended period of time. But when I'm reading a book, I'm fully engaged with the story, allowing me to develop my focus and concentration skills.

In conclusion, while social media platforms like TikTok may offer quick and easy entertainment, they can be a source of overstimulation and may not contribute to our personal growth and development. Instead, we should consider the benefits of reading and take the time to indulge in a good book. It may be a slower form of entertainment, but it can be much more rewarding in the long run.
